About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. student in the Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) department at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), advised by Prof. Naresh Shanbhag at the Coordinated Science Lab (CSL). Previously, I received my bachelor's degree from National Taiwan University (NTU), and my master's from UIUC. My research interests lie in VLSI for signal processing and communications, with a focus on developing efficient and high-performance circuits for communication systems. I was born as raised in the warm and beautiful port city of Kaohsiung, but spent a part of my childhood living in London, UK.
